User Guide

Getting Started

  1. Registration: Create an account with your username, password, personal details, and Gmail ID.
  2. Login: Use your username and password to access the system.
  3. Forgot Password: Use your Gmail ID and School/College name to recover your password.

Submitting a Diagnosis

  1. Navigate to the "Diagnosis" page from the menu.
  2. Fill in patient information (Name, Age, Contact Number).
  3. Enter vital signs (Temperature, Heart Rate, Blood Pressure, etc.).
  4. Optionally upload medical images (X-ray, MRI, skin images).
  5. Select an ML algorithm (Logistic Regression, SVM, CNN, or LSTM).
  6. Click "Submit Diagnosis" to get results.

Understanding Results

  • Green: Normal condition - no immediate action needed.
  • Yellow: Moderate condition - monitor and follow recommendations.
  • Red: Critical condition - seek immediate medical attention.

The results page shows a comparison chart of your vital signs vs. normal values.

Emergency Alerts

If your diagnosis shows critical results, the Emergency Alert page will automatically open with:

  • Emergency contact numbers
  • Nearby hospitals information
  • Emergency call buttons

Viewing History

  1. Go to the "History" page.
  2. Filter records by vital sign or date if needed.
  3. Export records as CSV or download individual PDF reports.

Health Recommendations

After submitting a diagnosis, visit the "Recommendations" page for personalized:

  • Diet suggestions
  • Exercise recommendations
  • Lifestyle modifications

Settings

  • Theme: Switch between light and dark mode.
  • Language: Choose between English and Tamil.
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and all data.

AI Chatbot

The chatbot in the bottom-right corner can help you with:

  • General questions about the system
  • Form guidance
  • Usage instructions
